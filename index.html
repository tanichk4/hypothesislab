Process

How it works

Three steps. No learning curve. No template wrangling.

Step 01
🧠
Describe your idea

Write anything — an observation, a hunch, a user complaint. No structure needed. Just the thought as it is in your head.

Step 02
🎯
Answer 2–3 focused questions

The service asks only what matters: who's affected, what behavior you expect, and what change you're looking for.

Step 03
✅
Get your hypothesis

Receive a structured "If → Then → Because" statement, clear success criteria, and a validation method matched to your product stage.

Deliverables

What you get

After one session with HypothesisLab.

◻
01 / Clean Hypothesis

A clean hypothesis in a standard format

No vague "maybe" or "it seems like." Your hypothesis lands in a precise If → Then → Because structure everyone on the team reads the same way.

◎
02 / Success Criteria

Concrete definition of "confirmed"

Know before you start what a win looks like. Clear, measurable criteria mean no post-hoc debates about whether the experiment worked.

▷
03 / Validation Method

The right method for your stage

Interview, A/B test, or smoke test — recommended based on where you are in the product cycle.

≡
04 / Hypothesis Log

Team memory of what you've tested

A running log tied to outcomes — so you never duplicate a failed experiment, and institutional knowledge stays in the product.
